Migraine

Migraine is a neurological condition characterized by recurrent moderate to severe headaches.

Common symptoms include:
- Throbbing head pain
- Sensitivity to light
- Sensitivity to sound
- Nausea
- Visual disturbances (aura)

Migraine episodes may last from several hours to several days.

Triggers may include:
- Stress
- Hormonal changes
- Lack of sleep
- Certain foods
