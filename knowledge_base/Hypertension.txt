Hypertension (High blood pressure)

Hypertension is a chronic condition characterized by persistently elevated blood pressure levels.

Common symptoms may include:
- Headaches
- Dizziness
- Shortness of breath
- Nosebleeds (in severe cases)
- Chest pain (in severe cases)

Risk factors include:
- Obesity
- High sodium diet
- Sedentary lifestyle
- Family history of hypertension
- Smoking

Hypertension is often referred to as a "silent condition" because many individuals experience no symptoms.
